A Sample Challenge E-mail

Once you install TMDA, your friends will receive a challenge e-mail the first time they write to you. Here's an example of what they will receive:

Date: Wed, 23 Apr 2003 00:13:13 -0500
From: You <your@email.addy>
To: Dave <daves@email.addy>
Subject: Please confirm your message

This message was created automatically by mail delivery software
(TMDA).

Your message attached below is being held because the address
<daves@email.addy> has not been verified.

To release your message for delivery, please send an empty message
to the following address, or use your mailer's "Reply" feature.

your-confirm-1051074793.30114.823365@email.addy

This confirmation verifies that your message is legitimate and not
junk-mail. You should only have to confirm your address once.
